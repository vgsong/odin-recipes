Soboro Don

A Classroom Recess Memory

Ingredients

  • Meat - ground pork or chicken
  • Vegetables - Ginger, Red Ginger, Peas, green onion
  • Eggs
  • Seasoning - Cooking Sake, Soy Sauce, Miring
  • White rice

Directions

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Cook meat, add ginger
  3. Deglaze with Mirin, Sake and Soy Sauce
  4. Remove cooked meat and cook eggs (preferably scrambled eggs)
  5. Serve with rice
