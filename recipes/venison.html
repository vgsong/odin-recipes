Venison Stew

Soul Delight

Ingredients

  • Meat - Cubed Beef for stew
  • Vegetables - Carrots, Potatoes, Onions, Garlic
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Seasoning - salt, pepper, bay leaf, oregano

Directions

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Cook meat, add vegetables
  3. Add three cups of water
  4. Cook on low/medium for 1 hour
  5. Enjoy
