Lasagna

Layers of Delight

Ingredients

  • Meat - Italian Sausage and lean ground beef
  • Vegetables - Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products - crushed tomatoes can
  • Sugar - Two tablespoon to add subtle sweetness
  • Seasoning - parsley, basil leaves, salt, black pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles - Store bought or handmade
  • Cheese - Parmesan, mozzarella
  • Meat

Directions

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Cook meat and add vegetables
  3. Add tomato sauce
  4. Boil lasagna noodles
  5. Add Spices/Seasoning
  6. Layer Meat Sauces/Cheese/Noodles
  7. Into preheated oven for 25 min
  8. Eat
